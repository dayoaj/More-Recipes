Recipe Title

  • Jollof Ricethumb_down thumb_up
Ingredients
  • 1 kg Ram
  • 1/2 Onion, chopped
  • 3 Tablespoons Pepper Soup Spice
  • 5 Medium sized Sweet Potato
  • 3 Carrots, sliced in circles
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Cameroon Pepper
  • 2 Goat Meat Seasoning Cube s
  • Handful of Scent Leaves, shredded
  • 3 Cups of Water
Instructions
  1. Cut Ram into byte sizes and wash.
  2. Transfer to a pot, add onion, salt, seasoning cube
  3. Stir to combine and cover it to steam.
  4. Do not add water yet, the ram would release it's own juices
  5. After 10 minutes, add pepper soup spices, carrots, sweet potato and 3 cups of water
  6. After 15 minutes, add chopped peppers and stir together
  7. Allow to cook for 5 more minutes
  8. Add scent leaves, turn off the cooker and allow to simmer.
  9. The steam will cook the scent leaves.
